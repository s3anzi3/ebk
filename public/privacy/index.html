Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

EBK ("Elite Ball Knowledge", "we", "us", "our") runs a free sports trivia arcade at eliteballknowledge.web.app (the "Service"). This policy explains, in plain language, what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices and rights you have. The short version: we collect only what we need to run accounts and leaderboards, we do not sell your data, and we run no advertising or third-party tracking.

1. Data Collection

We collect the following, and nothing more than we need:

  • Account information. When you create an account: your email address, a display name you choose, and a password. Your password is handled and secured by Firebase Authentication. We never see or store your plaintext password.
  • Username sign-in data. So you can sign in with your display name instead of your email, we store your email address encrypted with a key derived from your own password. Without your password, this stored value cannot be read by us or anyone else.
  • Gameplay data. Scores, best runs, play counts, daily grid results, and per-game statistics, tied to your account so leaderboards and your dashboard work.
  • Reports. If you report a display name, we store the report, the name reported, and your account identifier so we can review it.
  • On-device data. Best scores, sound settings, and similar preferences are stored in your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device.
  • Technical and log data. Our hosting provider (Google Firebase) records standard server logs such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps, which we use for security, abuse prevention, and reliability. We do not use this data to build advertising or marketing profiles.

2. Data Usage

We use your information only to operate and improve the Service:

  • Create and manage your account and keep you signed in.
  • Save your scores and power the public leaderboards and your personal dashboard.
  • Maintain fair play, review reports, and moderate display names.
  • Protect the Service through rate limiting and abuse prevention.
  • Send essential account emails, such as email verification and password resets.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

3. Cookies and Tracking

  • We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party tracking pixels.
  • We use your browser's local storage and the authentication tokens set by Firebase (stored locally on your device) to keep you signed in and remember your preferences and scores. These are strictly necessary for the Service to function.
  • We do not track you across other websites or services.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share data only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers. Google Firebase (Hosting, Authentication, and Cloud Firestore) processes data on our behalf to run the Service, under the Firebase privacy terms.
  • Public image servers. Player photos and team logos load from public sources (such as league sites and ESPN). Your browser requests those images directly, so those requests are subject to the relevant site's own policies.
  • Legal and safety. We may disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of EBK, our users, or the public.
  • Public by design. Your display name and your scores appear on public leaderboards. Your email address is never shown to other players.

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Wherever you live, we extend the following rights to you:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data (for example, change your display name in your dashboard).
  • Delete your account and associated data at any time.
  • Export / portability: request a copy of your account data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.

If you are in the European Economic Area or the UK, the GDPR gives you these rights, and you may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. If you are a California resident, the CCPA/CPRA gives you the right to know, delete, correct, and to not be discriminated against for exercising your rights. Because we do not sell or share personal information for advertising, there is nothing to opt out of on that front, but you may still delete your data at any time. To exercise any right, use the tools in your dashboard or contact us (see Contact). We will not charge you or deny you service for making a request.

6. Data Retention

  • We keep your account data for as long as your account is active.
  • When you delete your account (from your dashboard), we remove your account, scores, totals, daily-grid plays, and leaderboard entries. Residual copies in routine backups or server logs expire on their normal cycle shortly afterward.
  • Aggregate counts used for features like daily-grid rarity may be retained in a form that is not used to identify you personally.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and you must be at least 13 years old to create an account. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We keep the attack surface small: we never store plaintext passwords, your username-login email is encrypted with your password, traffic is served over HTTPS, database access is restricted by Firebase security rules, and score writes are validated and rate-limited. No method of transmission or storage is ever completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to protect your information.

9. Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions, or want to exercise a privacy right or delete your data? Open an issue at github.com/s3anzi3/ebk and we will respond promptly.
